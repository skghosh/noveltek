What We Offer

A full-spectrum transformation partner

From data strategy and AI enablement to cloud modernization and digital experience — we cover every layer of your digital transformation journey.

01
Data Strategy & Enablement

We align your data vision with your business goals — defining the architectures, operating models, and roadmaps that turn information into a strategic asset. From executive workshops to implementation blueprints, we help you build a data culture that drives smarter, faster decisions at every level of your organization.

Data Strategy
02
Enterprise Data Platform & Integration

We design and modernize enterprise data platforms across Microsoft Azure and Google Cloud Platform — delivering secure, scalable, and cost-optimized architectures in hybrid and multi-cloud environments. From batch and CDC to real-time streaming ingestion, we build a modern data foundation that connects your systems and powers enterprise-wide intelligence.

Data Platform
03
AI & Advanced Analytics

We architect and deploy operational AI applications across supply chain, manufacturing, logistics, and predictive maintenance. Our team manages the full ML lifecycle — from AI data readiness and feature engineering to MLOps, retraining pipelines, and production monitoring — so your AI investments generate real, measurable outcomes at scale.

AI / ML
04
Intelligent Automation

We unlock new levels of operational efficiency by integrating AI-driven insights with automation-powered workflows. From GenAI-powered process automation to RPA and intelligent decision engines, we help you eliminate repetitive tasks, reduce costs, and free your teams to focus on high-value work that moves the business forward.

Automation
05
Cloud-Native Modernization

We migrate and modernize legacy systems to cloud-native architectures using microservices, Kubernetes, Azure Functions, and Infrastructure as Code (Terraform). Our proven approach reduces licensing costs, accelerates deployment velocity, and positions your organization for long-term scalability across GCP, Azure, and AWS — whatever comes next.

Cloud
06
Insights & Activation

We go beyond reporting — delivering real-time intelligence, predictive analytics, and executive-grade BI dashboards using Looker, Power BI, BigQuery, Snowflake, and Databricks. We turn your analytics into action, helping planners, operators, and executives make high-confidence decisions faster and with greater precision.

Analytics
07
Data Management & Governance

We simplify governance and ensure data quality through solutions that secure, standardize, and scale your information assets. By establishing a Center of Excellence (CoE), architecture decision frameworks, and compliance-by-design practices, we ensure your data is trustworthy, auditable, and always fit for purpose.

Governance
08
Digital Experience & Application Services

We revitalize legacy applications and build modern, data-driven digital experiences that amplify engagement and improve business outcomes. From enterprise platform integrations and API-based microservices to mobile field applications and customer-facing portals, we deliver solutions built for performance, usability, and scale.

Digital

We started NovelTek Solutions in 2020 with a clear mission: make enterprise digital transformation accessible, practical, and lasting.

Today, NovelTek Solutions Inc. serves enterprises across industries, helping them reimagine their operations, accelerate innovation, and build technology that actually moves the needle.
